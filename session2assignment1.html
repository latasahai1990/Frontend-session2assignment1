Scrambled Eggs

Eggs are one of my favorite foods.Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tbs butter
  • 2 tbs cream

Method

  1. Melt a butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cream in the bowl
  3. Once butter has melted add cream and eggs
  4. Using a spatula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 ends (as if you are making an omelette).
  5. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served).
